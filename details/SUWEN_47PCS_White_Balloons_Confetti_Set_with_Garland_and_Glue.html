SUWEN 47PCS White Balloons and Confetti Set

This SUWEN white balloons set is a must-have for any special occasion. With 30 white latex balloons and 15 confetti balloons, you get a versatile mix to elevate your party decor. The set also includes a garland strip, dot glue, and silver ribbon, making it easy to arrange and secure the balloons without needing extra tools or time.

Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, graduation, or wedding, this decoration set adds a touch of sophistication and fun. The white balloons are ideal for creating a classic and elegant theme, while the confetti balloons add a vibrant, colorful accent. Perfect for creating memorable backdrops in photos, this set makes your event more visually appealing and heartwarming.
